Virus Bounce

Virus Bounce is an 8-bit, retro-style and endless-runner mobile game. Inspired by Covid.

To get further through this game, it's all about getting the right tempo on when to bounce-off and smash. To master this difficult game recognize the patterns and replay as many times as you want. Keep in mind, this game is very hard. If you're thinking about challenging your friends, you're at the right place! Get Virus Bounce now!

How to play? Just tap right-button (B) to bounce and dive-attack. Double bounce(B) to destroy the helmet wearing viruses. to smash and glide (Glide to increase air time) by pressing the left button (A) whilst on air. For every missed virus, 5 points will be deducted. Good luck! ;) What are you waiting for?! Go Smash 'em viruses!


Fall Ninja Shenanigans

Fall Ninja is a 3D mobile game where you can channel your inner ninja skills and get through fun challenges of spikey boxes, moving spikes and spinning obstacles. With over 100+ Levels to play and many ninja colors to unlock!

How to play? touch anywhere at the bottom half of the screen to move character to the right, you can touch-tap for short dashes, and hold-touch to leap for longer distance. You'll be faced by a series of obstacles that you have to avoid, you can be creative on how to get through these obstacles, just remember, avoid the black spikey boxes and the spinning obstacle, and try not to fall! because the white boxes will fall once bounced-on. Have fun!
